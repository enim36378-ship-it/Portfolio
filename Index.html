Technology and Machine Learning

Technology is evolving rapidly and changing the way we live, work, and communicate. Machine Learning, a part of Artificial Intelligence, allows systems to learn from data and improve automatically without being explicitly programmed.

From self-driving cars to voice assistants, Machine Learning plays a key role in modern technology. It is shaping industries like healthcare, finance, and education.
